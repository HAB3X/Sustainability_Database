    Company overview

    ALS Limited is a global testing laboratories company headquartered in Brisbane. It provides analytical testing, inspection, certification and verification services for clients in mining, environmental monitoring, water quality, food safety, pharmaceuticals, energy, manufacturing and public health. Its laboratories are used by governments, large corporations and regulators to ensure compliance, safety and scientific integrity.

    The company traces its origins to 1863 and has grown from a local Brisbane enterprise into a global scientific services organisation through organic expansion and acquisitions. ALS positions itself as a provider of independent and reliable analytical data that supports evidence based decision making in regulated and technical industries.

    Divisions and operations

    Minerals

    • Geochemistry, metallurgy and mineral processing services for exploration and production.
    • Mine site laboratory services, inspection, sampling and commodities certification.
    • Serves global mining and exploration clients across major resource regions.

    Life Sciences

    • Environmental testing for soil, water, air and waste.
    • Food and beverage testing, microbiology and quality assurance.
    • Pharmaceutical and drinking water compliance analysis in selected markets.
    • Human health diagnostics and related testing in some regions.

    Energy

    • Testing and analysis of oil, gas and coal quality.
    • Fuel and lubricant condition monitoring for engines and industrial equipment.
    • Services that support asset reliability in energy and heavy industry.

    Industrial

    • Asset care, inspection and engineering services.
    • Non destructive testing and condition monitoring.
    • Support for manufacturing, infrastructure, defence and aerospace clients.

    Global presence

    ALS operates a broad network of laboratories and offices with more than 350 locations across around 70 countries. It has significant operations in Australia and New Zealand, North America, Latin America, Europe, the Middle East and Asia. This footprint allows ALS to provide local laboratory support while maintaining global quality systems and data standards.

    The company often enters new markets by acquiring local laboratories, integrating them into the ALS network and aligning them with group quality, safety and compliance frameworks.

    Sustainability and key practices

    Environmental testing and public interest role

    • Provides testing that underpins environmental regulation, including analysis of soil, sediment and groundwater contamination.
    • Supports monitoring of drinking water safety, wastewater discharge and broader aquatic ecosystem health.
    • Conducts specialised testing for emerging contaminants such as per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances and microplastics.

    Corporate environmental footprint

    • Reports environmental and energy metrics in its annual and sustainability disclosures.
    • Runs programmes to improve laboratory energy efficiency and reduce resource use where feasible.
    • Pursues digitalisation and automation to reduce paper based processes and improve sample throughput.

    Ethics, data integrity and quality

    • Implements strict protocols for chain of custody, sample handling and data reporting.
    • Many laboratories are accredited to recognised standards such as ISO or NATA where applicable.
    • Emphasises independence and objectivity in analytical results as part of its value proposition.

    People and safety

    • Employs thousands of laboratory professionals, technicians and engineers across the globe.
    • Operates in environments that require strong safety practices due to chemical, biological and physical hazards.
    • Invests in training and career development to maintain technical competence and retain skilled staff.
