    Company overview

    APA Group is an energy infrastructure business that owns and operates critical gas and electricity networks across Australia. The company‚Äôs assets transport natural gas, provide gas storage and processing, support electricity transmission, and include a growing portfolio of renewable generation and battery energy storage projects.

The group also invests in energy infrastructure and provides specialist asset management and operational services to both its own investments and third party owners.
