    Company overview

    Ampol Limited is a major Australian fuel supplier involved in refining, importing, distributing and marketing petroleum fuels across Australia and New Zealand.

    It previously operated under the Caltex brand before adopting the historic Ampol name again following the end of the Chevron licence agreement.

    Business segments

    Fuels and infrastructure

    • Manages crude supply, shipping and refining.
    • Operates Lytton Refinery in Brisbane.
    • Owns fuel terminals, depots and pipelines.
    • Supplies fuel to defence, mining and transport sectors.

    Convenience retail

    • Operates Ampol service stations and Foodary stores.
    • Provides retail-facing fuel and convenience operations.

    Z Energy (New Zealand)

    • Major fuel retailer across New Zealand.
    • Truck stops, service stations, and commercial supply.

    Emerging energy

    • AmpCharge EV charging network rollout.
    • Commercial fleet transition services.
    • Explores hydrogen, renewable diesel and SAF.

    Operations and infrastructure

    Ampol operates a national network of infrastructure including pipelines, depots, terminals and marine facilities. Lytton Refinery remains a major Australian refining asset.
