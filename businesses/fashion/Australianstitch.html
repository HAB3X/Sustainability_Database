Australian Stitch

    Company Overview

    Australian Stitch is a Melbourne based clothing maker and retailer specialising in locally manufactured basics. The company produces tees, hoodies, jumpers, and accessories, operating a Fitzroy shopfront and manufacturing facility in Thornbury. It promotes sustainable and ethical production within the Australian textile industry.

    Sustainability and Key Practices

    • Local manufacturing with fair wages and safe working conditions.
    • Ethical sourcing and small batch production to reduce waste.
    • Durable basics designed for longevity rather than fast fashion trends.
    • Partnerships with local dye houses and material suppliers to support the regional rag trade.
    • Focus on transparency, community connection, and responsible consumption.
