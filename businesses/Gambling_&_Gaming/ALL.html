    Company overview

    Aristocrat Leisure Limited is a global gaming content and technology company with operations across land based gambling machines, social casino style mobile games and regulated real money online gambling. The group supplies electronic gaming machines, casino management systems, iLottery content and a large portfolio of digital games.

    The company is one of the largest suppliers of poker machines in Australia and a major participant in casinos and gaming venues internationally. Through its digital businesses, Aristocrat publishes social casino apps and operates regulated online real money gaming platforms, extending gambling style products to mobile and online environments.

    Business segments

    Aristocrat Gaming

    • Designs, develops and manufactures electronic gaming machines for casinos, clubs and hotels.
    • Provides casino management systems and gaming platforms to land based operators.

    Product Madness (social casino and mobile games)

    • Publishes free to play social casino games for mobile and online platforms.
    • Generates revenue through in app purchases and virtual currency rather than traditional wagering.
    • Uses behavioural design and live operations to drive player engagement and spending.

    Aristocrat Interactive (real money online gaming)

    • Provides regulated real money online gambling solutions and content.
    • Includes iLottery content and online casino style games where permitted by law.
    • Operates in a tightly regulated environment with licencing obligations in multiple jurisdictions.
