Welcome to the Sustainability Database

    The Sustainability Database is a public, open-source platform dedicated to cataloguing sustainable businesses across Australia and New Zealand. It supports UN Sustainable Development Goal 4 (Quality Education) by making sustainability practices transparent, accessible, and free to use for consumers, students, educators, and researchers.

    Our aim is to build a knowledge base that supports ethical enterprise and encourages informed, responsible decision-making throughout the economy.

    About Us

    We organise businesses into broader umbrella sectors for simplicity and clarity:

    • Consumer & Lifestyle — Fashion, Health & Wellness, Food & Beverage, Home & Furniture
    • Infrastructure & Environment — Construction & Real Estate, Energy, Agriculture
    • Mobility & Travel — Transport, Travel & Tourism, Automotive
    • Finance & Governance — Finance & Banking, Government
    • Education & Culture — Education, Entertainment, Events & Conferences
    • Other — Pets, Sport

    This site is collaboratively maintained through GitHub. Submissions are peer-reviewed, and businesses listed must demonstrate verifiable sustainable practices. The project is entirely open-source and prioritises public education and shared knowledge.
